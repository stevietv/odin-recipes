Caramel Shortcake - Recipe

Description

A nice traditional treat with shortcake, caramel and chocolate!

Ingredients

  • 120 gram unsalted butter
  • 55 grams sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • 180 grams self raising flour
  • 60 grams unsalted butter
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 4 tbps brown sugar
  • 200 grams milk chocolate
  • 40 grams white chocolate

Steps

The base

  1. Mix the butter, sugar and salt to a creamy mixture
  2. Add the flour is parts and mix well
  3. Grease a roud baking tin and fill it with the dough. Press down and prick holes in it with a fork
  4. Bake at 180C for 20 minutes

The caramel

  1. Melt the butter in a pan
  2. Add the condensed milk and brown sugar. Keep stirring until it disolves
  3. Increase the heat and keep stirring for 10 minutes
  4. Spread this over the cooled base and refrigerate until set

The topping

  1. Melt the milk chocolate in a bain marie
  2. When this is melted, set it aside and melt the white chocolate in a piping sack in the microwave.
  3. Pour the milk chocolate over the base
  4. Pipe the white chocolate in your desired pattern on top
