Black Bean Soup - Recipe

Description

This scrummy black bean soup with bacon bits is great for a cold winter evening.

When served with crunchy baguette and sour cream it rounds of the meal perfectly

Ingredients

  • 1 pack diced bacon
  • 1 onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 4 cans black beans (drained)
  • 500ml Chicken Broth
  • 1 can chopped tomatos
  • Chilli powder
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • tomato ketchup
  • lime juice

Steps

  1. Fry up the bacon in a pot
  2. After cooked, add onions and cook until translucent (4 minutes)
  3. Add garlic and cook 4 more minutes
  4. Add Chili powder, Worcestershire sauce, and a dollop of ketchup
  5. Add chicken broth and stir
  6. Add Tomatoes and Black Beans
  7. Simmer for about 15 - 20 minutes
  8. Add lime and cilantro to taste
  9. Serve with sour cream or creme fraiche
